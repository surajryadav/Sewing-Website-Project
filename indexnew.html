    What is Sewing Machine?

    A sewing machine is a machine used to stitch fabrics, clothes and materials together with thread. Sewing machines were invented during the 1st industrial revolution that started in 1960 under Planning Commission setup by then Prime Minister Jawahar Lal Nehru to help the workers working in clothing companies. These are machines that are meant for industry and are used in factories around the world that need to sew materials together. Most relevant for us is the garment manufacturing industry as that is the industry that fashion and clothing design falls into. Tables which provide space for the product being sewn while the machines themselves are built for durability, reliability and speed.
